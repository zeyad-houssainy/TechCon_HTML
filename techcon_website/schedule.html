Detailed Schedule of Events for TechCon 2024
Time Session Speaker(s) Room Description
Day 1: Friday, October 25, 2024 - Innovation Day
9:00 AM - 9:45 AM Opening Keynote: The AI Revolution Dr. Anya Sharma Main Auditorium A deep dive into the ethical implications and future potential of artificial intelligence.
10:00 AM - 10:45 AM Building Scalable Cloud Solutions Marcus Chen Room 101 Best practices for designing and deploying robust cloud infrastructure.
11:00 AM - 11:45 AM Sustainable Tech: Green Innovations Sophia Rodriguez Room 102 Exploring eco-friendly approaches in software and hardware development.
11:45 AM - 1:00 PM Lunch Break & Networking Exhibition Hall Opportunity to enjoy lunch and connect with peers and exhibitors.
1:00 PM - 1:45 PM Cybersecurity in the Quantum Age Dr. Lena Petrova Room 101 Understanding and preparing for post-quantum cryptographic threats.
2:00 PM - 2:45 PM Web3 and Decentralized Applications Alex 'Blockchain' Kim Room 103 An introduction to the decentralized web and dApp development.
3:00 PM - 3:45 PM Interactive UI/UX Design Workshop Sarah Lee Workshop Room A Hands-on session covering modern UI/UX principles and tools.
4:00 PM - 4:45 PM Panel Discussion: The Future of Work in Tech Various Speakers Main Auditorium Leaders discuss trends, challenges, and opportunities in the evolving tech workforce.
5:00 PM - 6:00 PM Closing Reception & Happy Hour Exhibition Hall Wind down with drinks and appetizers, further networking opportunities.
Day 2: Saturday, October 26, 2024 - Development & Discovery Day
9:00 AM - 9:45 AM Keynote: Quantum Computing Breakthroughs Prof. David Miller Main Auditorium Latest advancements and practical applications of quantum computing.
10:00 AM - 10:45 AM Machine Learning for Beginners Dr. Emily White Room 102 An introductory guide to machine learning concepts and basic models.
11:00 AM - 11:45 AM DevOps: Accelerating Software Delivery Carlos "The Automator" Ruiz Room 101 Strategies and tools for efficient and continuous software deployment.
11:45 AM - 1:00 PM Lunch Break & Tech Demos Exhibition Hall Explore live tech demonstrations while enjoying lunch.
1:00 PM - 1:45 PM AR/VR: Immersive Experiences Jessica "Jinx" Wu Room 103 Designing and developing augmented and virtual reality applications.
2:00 PM - 2:45 PM Data Science: From Raw Data to Insights Dr. Ben Carter Room 102 Techniques for data analysis, visualization, and predictive modeling.
3:00 PM - 3:45 PM Building Cross-Platform Mobile Apps Chloe Green Workshop Room B Practical guide to developing mobile applications for iOS and Android using modern frameworks.
4:00 PM - 4:45 PM Closing Keynote: The Human Element in Future Tech Dr. Anya Sharma Main Auditorium Reflecting on how technology can enhance human potential and creativity.
4:45 PM - 5:00 PM Conference Wrap-up & Farewell TechCon Team Main Auditorium Final remarks and thank you from the TechCon organizers.
